The next best thing since sliced bread. The old Baloney and Cheese sandwich. A Canadian staple.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Baloney
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Mustard
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Take 2 slices of bread
  2. Add baloney, lettuce and cheese to bread
  3. Spread mustard on one side of the empty slice of bread and place over other half (facedown)
